Founders

Noah Monyak

Noah Monyak is a CVCA student, graphic designer, musician, and wallball player. Noah came up with the NWA logo, website, and brand.

Isaac Solomon

Isaac Solomon, CVCA student, was the spark of the wallball at CVCA. A competitive and talented wallball player, Isaac holds a strong spot on the founder team. Isaac was the main writer of the official NWA wallball rules. Isaac is always winning.

Moses Bader

Moses Bader is a CVCA student, and arguably one of the funniest in the founder group. Moses is always bringing the joy and laughter to wallball. Moses also played a large part in making the official NWA wallball roles fair and just.

Noah Lancy

Noah Lancy is a CVCA student, baseball player, and relaxed wallball player. Holding the record for most second places in the NWA, you can find Noah throwing 1v1s often. In all seriousness, Noah is a great mediator and brings the peace to wallball games. With great knowledge of the rules, Noah can end debates quickly and keep the game moving.
